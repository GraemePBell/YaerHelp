Version 1.0.19.0

Bug fixes and performance improvements, including:

  • Correct handling of Standard Ebooks Advanced epub format book covers. These beautifully curated Ebooks use state-of-the-art ereader and browser technologies to present a professional-grade reading experience, with pubic-domain, fine-art SVG-format covers.
